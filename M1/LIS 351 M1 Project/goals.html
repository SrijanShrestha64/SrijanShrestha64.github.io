Introduction

This page will be about some goals I want to achieve in my life. Some of them will be in the near future, but others will be more long term. I think that setting goals is important because it keeps you accountable, and is a good way to track how you are doing.

My Goals

  • Getting a good job after graduation. With the current state of the job market this is probably my number one goal right now. Getting a stable job will also help me with my other goals.
  • Learn a new language. I am already fluent in Nepali and English, and I think it would be cool to know 3 languages. Spanish would be the third language I learn because I like Spanish music, and I took Spanish in high school. I did forget a lot but it would be easier than starting from scratch.
  • Continue focusing on health. During college I have focused on my health and made it a priority. Whether that be with diet, exercise, sleep, or mental health. I think as I get older it will be harder and harder to focus on health so building a good foundation now is very important to me and this is a lifelong goal.
