Introduction

In my free time I like to spend a lot of time with friends and family. Activities that can be done with others tend to be my favorite and are usually what I opt for. I also like to have some hobbies that I do just for myself that allow me to relax and take a break from school. Another important part of my free time is learning, so I always try to have a new skill that I am slowly teaching myself.

Hobby List

  • Soccer – Playing soccer with friends, and in intermural leagues.
  • Working out – Weightlifting for hypertrophy and general health.
  • Drawing – I've always drew here and there but now I am actively learning how to get better

Details About My Hobbies

Soccer
I have been playing soccer for 16 years, and played competitively from 4th grade to 12th grade. After high school ended I stopped playing as frequently but I still play weekly with friends whether is pick up, or an intermural league. When I played competitively I was a veritile player but mainly played as a right wing back, but now I enjoy playing right wing. I find soccer so fun because you can express yourself on the field.
Working out
I technically started working out in middle school, as it was a part of my soccer training, but I would say I really started the summer after my senior year of high school, so 2 years. During this time I focused on weight lifting. I somtimes workout with friends if I see them in the gym, but usually I like to go solo. I like weightlifting because it is fun seeing progress throughout the months and years.
Drawing
As most kids do, I have been drawing ever since I was a toddler but quarentine is when I started to try to get better. I mainly like drawing realistic landscapes but recently I have been trying to get better at drawing people. I draw because I think its a good outlet for when life gets hectic. It is always accessible, easy to set up, and you don't need to commit a large amout of time to it.
